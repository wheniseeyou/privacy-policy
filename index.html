Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 17, 2026

App: HealthCare+ - Your Discipline, Health Agent

1. Health Data (HealthKit / CareKit)

This app accesses your health data through Apple HealthKit and CareKit frameworks, including but not limited to: steps, workout distance, active energy, heart rate, weight, height, body fat percentage, sleep data, nutrition intake, HRV (heart rate variability).

Data Handling:

  • All health data is stored locally on your device only.
  • We do not upload health data to any external server without your explicit consent.
  • We do not use health data for advertising, marketing, or sell it to any third party.
  • Health data is used solely to provide personal health tracking and analysis services.

Data Synchronization: If you enable iCloud sync, health-related data is encrypted and synced through Apple CloudKit. The sync process is protected by Apple iCloud privacy terms. We as the developer cannot access your iCloud data content.

2. Other Data Collection

Account Information: If you use CloudKit anonymous login, Apple provides us with an anonymous user identifier (User Record ID) to distinguish data between different users. We cannot identify your real identity from this.

Usage Data: We do not collect any analytics data, crash reports, or user behavior tracking.

3. Data Security

Health data is protected by iOS system-level encryption. This app does not request network permissions to transmit data to proprietary servers.

4. Your Rights

You may revoke the app's health data access permissions at any time in iOS Settings - Privacy & Security - Health. After revoking permissions, the app will be unable to read or write HealthKit data, but this does not affect already stored local data.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions, please contact us through the App Store Connect contact module.

6. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy. The updated policy will be published within the App or on the App Store page, and the effective date will be updated accordingly.

This privacy policy is available in 8 languages. The English version is the authoritative version.
